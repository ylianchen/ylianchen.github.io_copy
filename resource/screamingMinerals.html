 YCH 
Y.Liang Chen

Excavated sonic potentials from discarded matter through construction of hand-built oscillating instruments from electronic waste and radio-sensitive minerals. Performed in an improvised orchestra where tactile interventions activated technological residue, generating unpredictable sound fields. The work traced historical connections between primitive crystal radio technologies and contemporary computational systems while reanimating technological detritus as instruments of resistance. (Developed within the "Sizzling Semiconductors" workshop framework, conducted by Ioana Vreme Moser).
